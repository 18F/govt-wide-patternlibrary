What is TechFAR Hub?

In the TechFAR Hub you will be able to learn everything you need to plan and execute a digital service acquisition. You will also find rescources to engage with the Acquisition Community to ask questions and share your experiences to help others. Finally, the TechFAR Hub provides powerful tools and templates to get you started on your very own Digital Service Acquisition!

Browser support:

The standards are designed to support both older and newer browsers through progressive enhancement, and officially support Internet Explorer 9 and up, and the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, and Safari. Internet Explorer 8 and below generally see very low usage today, and most agency websites should be able to safely begin support at Internet Explorer 9.
